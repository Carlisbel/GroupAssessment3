JOB 1

WEB DESIGNER

As a Web Designer, you’ll take the lead in working with our Team to conceptualise, workshop, and finalise beautiful, user-centred visual designs for websites. This role will report to the “IT co-ordinator and be part of a squad with visual design and web development skills. The position is now and will continue to be a fully remote work-from-home position within Australia.

What you'll do:
  • Produce high-quality visual designs (page layouts, templates)
  • Facilitate design workshops with clients and articulate design decisions to stakeholders
  • Facilitate mutually beneficial decision-making
  • Communicate user-centred methods and their value to non-designers
  • Provide effective and timely feedback to peers and designers
Who you are:
  • A problem solver with exceptional communication ability
  • Have a portfolio showcasing design work for the web
  • At least 2 years’ experience in designing for content management systems
  • Hands-on experience with Figma, Adobe , Jira, GitHub
  • Strong command of colour theory, image composition, and typography
  • Preferably with experience developing with HTML/CSS/JavaScript

Server-side
